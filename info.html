Innovate To Bring Down Unemployment In India


In this era of the digital world, we have a life-changing opportunity to bring down unemployment in India. Stats say that literate people contribute maximum to unemployment in our country. This means that most of this young unemployed crowd can read and write. Not just that they are all aware of the digital world and world of apps as most of the population is on social media platforms like Facebook and WhatsApp.  

The above information will help us in bringing down unemployment in our country. We will use Digital Marketing to help people earn and feed themselves and their family.

Why Digital Marketing?



Well as we already know that these literate people are unemployed, which means that there are not sufficient jobs out there to help them. Also starting a brick and mortar business needs huge financial capital. So to solve all these issues we use digital marketing and this can be easily started even with a mobile phone which mostly all people have and to excel in this digital marketing world, we would just need a laptop and an internet connection which is again very affordable for anyone.

Do they need any special skills?



No, they don’t need any special skills. Well, they can start this month or even today as the opportunities are huge and anyone can do this without any prior experience or skills. They can turn their passions into a full-time living using digital media. For Example, if someone is good at Yoga, they can start yoga classes online and earn. If someone is good at teaching English, they can start their classes online. If someone is good at creating videos, they can start a YouTube channel and earn. If someone is good at writing, then they can sell kindle on Amazon.

How will this help our country?



This model will not only help us bring down unemployment and make anyone self-sufficient but also will greatly benefit our financial economy as all incomes are transferred digitally, so it will increase the total taxpayer number in the country. This means the government will receive more taxes and that will further improve the infrastructure of the country as well as will boost our economy.
