What Is Unemployment?


Unemployment is a term referring to individuals who are employable and actively seeking a job but are unable to find a job. In very simple words, people who are jobless or rather ones who lost their job due to covid Eventhough, this pandemic teaches us a lot- personal hygiene, social distance etc, it hasn't been good for many out there.





Impact On MSME


1. The whole shutdown at the months of april and may, the movement of people blocked caused people to hardly survive.

2. People due to the fear of covid shifted to their hometowns so as to be for their families and this caused lack of labour.

Reasons Of Unemployment


1. The main reason for unemployment was the lockdown, many companies didn’t approve the concept of work from home as the presence of the worker was important and since the lockdown was imposed the movement was blocked.

2. Many companies fired employees since the company was on downfall not thinking about their employees.

3. Travel SECTORS is one of the sector WHICH FACED A TREMENDOUS AMOUNT OF LOSS.
